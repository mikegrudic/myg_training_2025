Elevation Profile Pace Calculator

Upload a GPX track to see elevation, grade, and Strava grade-adjusted-pace factor vs. distance, assuming a constant-fatigue-rate model where the rate of work you are doing decays by a certain percentage each hour. Note that the GAP model is calibrated to highly idealized conditions (not real trails) and is simply extrapolated to any absolute grades greater than 30%.
